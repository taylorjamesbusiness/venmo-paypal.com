💡 How to Pay with PYUSD

✕
1
Open PayPal → Go to Finances tab
Tap Crypto Balance → find PayPal USD → tap the ⇄ transfer arrows button at the bottom right.
2
Tap "Send PYUSD"
A popup "More crypto actions" will appear — tap Send PYUSD.
3
Paste the Copied Address
Paste the PYUSD address copied from this page. A popup "You're sending on Solana" will appear — tap Got it & click the address.
4
Set the Exact Amount
Enter the exact amount shown on this invoice. You can specify in PYUSD or your local fiat currency equivalent → tap Next.
5
Review & Tap "Send Now"
Confirm Network: Solana, check the amount → tap Send Now. Small network fee (~$0.14) applies.
6
Done! Payment Confirmed
Solana confirms in ~5 minutes. You have 1 hour before this invoice expires.
