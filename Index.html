How to pay

1. Choose PayPal or Venmo

Select the payment method from the homepage and create the invoice.

2. Open the app

Open PayPal or Venmo and go to the crypto section.

3. Select PYUSD

If PYUSD balance is 0, first buy the same amount of PYUSD you want to pay.

4. Tap Send

Paste the invoice address and confirm that the network matches the invoice.

5. Confirm payment

Send the correct amount and wait for payment status to update.

Integration notes

  • This frontend calls a public backend invoice endpoint and then polls your invoices table for status.
  • The backend endpoint should create the wallet or invoice server-side and return invoice data to the browser.
  • Do not put any Railway private token, service-role key, or wallet secret in this file.
  • If your DB column names differ, update the mapping in normalizeInvoiceRow().

Payment guide

How to pay with PYUSD

Step 1

Select PayPal or Venmo and create the invoice from the first page.

Step 2

Open the PayPal or Venmo app and go to the Crypto section.

Step 3

Choose PYUSD. If your PYUSD balance is 0, first buy the same amount of PYUSD you want to pay.

Step 4

Tap Send, paste the invoice address, and make sure the network matches the invoice.

Step 5

Enter the correct amount and confirm the payment. Wait for the invoice status to change.

Wrong address or wrong network can cause permanent loss of funds.
