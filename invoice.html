💡 How to Pay

1
Copy the PYUSD address
Tap "Copy Address" button below the QR code on this page.
2
Open PayPal or Venmo
Tap the "Go to PayPal" or "Go to Venmo" button to open the app.
3
Check your PYUSD balance
Go to your Crypto section. If your PYUSD balance is $0, first buy PYUSD or convert from your existing balance.
4
Send to the copied address
Select Send PYUSD → External Wallet, paste the copied address, enter the exact amount , and confirm.
5
Wait for confirmation
Payment will be confirmed automatically. You have 1 hour before this invoice expires.
